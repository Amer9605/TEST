The Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification is an entry-level credential that validates a professional's ability to install, configure, operate, and troubleshoot medium-sized routed and switched networks. It covers essential networking concepts such as IP addressing, LAN/WAN technologies, routing protocols, network security, and infrastructure services. The CCNA is widely recognized in the IT industry, serving as a foundational certification for networking careers and a prerequisite for more advanced Cisco certifications.
